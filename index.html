Semiotica
Semiotica is an interactive modular typeface built for playful semiotics. By toggling the modules at different angles, one finds themselves wander in a maze of recognizability and unfamiliarity that form and break letterforms. By dragging the circular knobs, one can also stack and connect the groups of shapes that form new symbols. Eventually, the page becomes a digital tapestry of patterns that once make up letterforms.
This typeface is designed by Selina Kehuan Wu in Interaction Capstone, Fall 2021. Title set in FK Raster Grotesk by Florian Karsten; body text set in Lausanne by Weltkern Type Foundry.
